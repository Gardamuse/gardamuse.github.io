Stereotypically, bimbos add many unneccessary words when they speak, and they are not very good at spelling either. This app lets you transform any text into something that a bimbo might have written.

Stereotypically, bimbos add, like, absolutely kinda uh, many unecesary words weh they speak, like, anb they're, like, nt vey u knw, god as speling eider. Ths app lets you transform, like, any text ino something dat a bimbo might have, like, writen.
