A quick guide to Texas Holdem Poker

In short, the only mechanic is betting. You bet more if you think your hand is better and less if you think your hand is worse than that of other players. A hand always has 5 cards and how good it is can be found out from this list of poker hands.

In Texas Hold'em you first only get 2 cards and bet how good you think your hand is. Then 3 cards are added to the table for everyone to see. Now your hand consists of these 3 + the original 2 = 5 cards, and you bet again. Then another card is added so there are now 2 + 4 = 6 cards. Your hand is now the set of 5 cards (out of these 6) that is the best and you bet again. In the same way, another card is added so there are now 2 + 5 = 7 cards and your hand is again the set of 5 cards (out of these 7) that is the best. You bet a final time and all players who have not decided to stop betting show their cards and the player with the best hand wins.

Raising is just that you think your hand are good and want to bet more. Folding means that you think your hand is bad and want to stop betting. Calling is the in-between where you bet just the minimum amount to stay in the game.
