Background

For a while I have been pondering the lack of good bimbo quizzes. I've found some, but very few are any good. So, with inspiration from Kaokatt's Who's Your Inner Bimbo!?! flow chart and MythicalCreatureBone's Eliminated (page 2, 3, 4a, 4b), I decided to make my own.

Online quizzes are often very arbitrary, and so is this one in that I have made up what each bimbo type would be likely to answer on each question. However, aside from that, this quiz uses a proper statistical model in order to calculate the likelihood of having a particular personality. This means that as long as my assumptions on different bimbo types are reasonable, the classification will be much more accurate than your typical online quiz.
