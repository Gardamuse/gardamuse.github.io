The two videos on this page were created by the youtube channel ChannelBulbus in 2008. Unfortunately the original videos seems to have been removed by youtube in 2019. In addition, despite having kept my eye out for the artist for over a decade now, I've never found any more of ChannelBulbus' work or any trace of what they are doing now.

Channebulbus is in some sense a hero of mine, and these two videos are what made me search out the breast expansion art community in the first place. Therefore, and because it would be a shame to have such excellent videos dissappear, I've decided to host them here for the time being.

ChannelBulbus, wherever you are, I hope you are doing well!
